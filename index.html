About Us

Bawz PH was founded in 2019 by a group of individuals united by the same raw style and vision, led by Aennon Tabungar .

What started as a small collective has grown into a powerful movement. Today, Directed by Aennon Tabungar, Bawz is home to 80+ dancers , each bringing their own flavor, story, and energy—yet moving as one.

Events

More than performances, our events create spaces for dancers to grow, connect, and be seen — from community classes to full-scale showcases and international qualifiers.

Bawz Complex Community Classes

December 27–28, 2025 | Bawz Complex

A community-driven, pay-what-you-can dance event created to make movement accessible to everyone. Over two full days, Bawz PH hosted back-to-back classes featuring multiple styles and levels, allowing dancers of all backgrounds to learn, grow, and connect in one shared space.

Bawz Stage: Volume 3

July 26, 2025 | SM Skydome

Bawz Stage Volume 3 was a full-scale dance concert showcasing Bawz PH's versatility through original pieces across different styles. The night also featured performances from various dance teams in the community, turning the stage into a celebration of creativity, collaboration, and raw performance energy.

SummerJam Showcase Competition Philippine Qualifier

November 16, 2024 | Henry Lee Irwin Theater

Bawz PH proudly organized the Philippine Qualifier for the SummerJam Showcase Competition, an international dance franchise originating in Vietnam. The event gathered top local teams competing for a chance to represent the Philippines on the global stage against international contenders.
